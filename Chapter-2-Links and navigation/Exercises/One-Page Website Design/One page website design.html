Overview

High school students face a desperate mission to flee their contaminated institution which now serves as the base for a zombie virus disaster. The SSouth Korean television series Squid Game combines survival elements with debt-ridden characters who compelete against children's games while fighting for life to win big cash.The series investigates matters related to essential needs, social inequality and the behavioral tendencies of human beings.
