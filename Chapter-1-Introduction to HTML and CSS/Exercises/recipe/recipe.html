Nihari

Nihari is a classic slow-cooked meat stew begin from the indian subcontinent. It is a tasty and spicy dish made with lamb or beef, seethe overnight with savoury spices. Regularly served with roti or paratha, it's a favorite in Pakistan and North Indian most liked.

Ingredients

  • 750g beef shank (with bone)
  • 1 cup cooking oil or ghee
  • 1 large onions, delicately sliced
  • 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 3 tsp Wheat flour (For Thickening)
  • 4 tsp Nihari masala
  • 1 tsp or to taste Red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 2 litre water or as required
  • Fresh coriander leaves, garnish or chopped
  • Ginger(julienne) Garnishing
  • Green chilli(sliced)
  • Lemon wedges(optional)

Directions

  1. Heat cooking oil or ghee in a large pot. Fry sliced onions umtil golden brown.
  2. In a pot, add cooking oil and beef shank then mix well and cook on medium flame for 4-5 minutes.
  3. Add gingr garlic paste, mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Now add red chilli powder, Turmeric powder and Nihari masala and mix well to coat the meat with spices.
  5. Add water,mix well and bring it to boil,cover and cook on low flame for 1 hour and 30 minutes or until meat is tender and stir in between (if required water).
  6. Mix wheat flour with a little water to make a smooth slurry. Add to the pot to thicken the gravy.
  7. Turn of the flame,cover and let it rest for 10 minutes.
  8. At the end, Garnish with Fresh chopped coriander leaves,ginger,green chilli,lemon and serve.
